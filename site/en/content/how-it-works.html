How EasyRead.me Works

Text Analysis

Our system analyzes your text using natural language processing to identify complex words, lengthy sentences, and technical jargon that might be difficult to understand.

Simplification Process

We use advanced AI algorithms to:

  • Replace complex words with simpler alternatives
  • Break down long sentences into shorter ones
  • Explain technical terms in plain language
  • Maintain the original meaning while improving readability

Quality Assurance

Each simplified text is checked for:

  • Readability score improvements
  • Grammar and spelling accuracy
  • Meaning preservation
  • Cultural sensitivity
