Privacy Policy

Privacy and Your Information

We care about your privacy.
We protect your personal information.

Collecting Information

We collect the information you give us and the results we provide back to you.

Storing Information

We keep your information safe in a secure place for a short time.
This helps us provide our service and improve quality.

Sharing Information

We share the information with our service providers: Microsoft and Mistral AI.
We do not share your information with other users.
We also do not use your information to train our services.
If you want to learn more, please see the following pages:

  • Microsoft's data, privacy and security page
  • Mistral AI's privacy policy

Avoid Sharing Sensitive Information

Despite all those precautions, we advise not to enter sensitive information on the platform:

  • Do not enter personal details like your name, address, bank information
  • Do not enter confidential material like business secrets
  • Do not enter copyrighted content
