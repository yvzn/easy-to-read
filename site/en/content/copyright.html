Copyright Information

Content Ownership

When you use EasyRead.me:

  • You retain all applicable rights to your original content
  • Simplified versions are derivative works under your copyright
  • We claim no ownership over your content or simplified versions

Copyright issues on generated content

Generated content is made from scratch and not copied from other sources.

Sometimes, generated content might appear similar to other content.
This can happen by accident because of the large amount of information the AI has learned from.

If you think any generated content is too similar to existing content, please reach out to us.
We want to solve any problems and ensure that all content follows copyright laws.
