Sustainability Commitment

Understanding AI Uses Energy

We know that using artificial intelligence (AI) and large language models uses a lot of energy.
This requires powerful computers, which can harm the environment.

Minimizing AI Impact

We are using smaller AI models that are less harmful to the environment.
This means we choose models that use less computer power and energy.
Also, we keep our AI models in data centers that are in the most carbon-efficient areas.
By doing this, we hope to lower our impact on the environment.

Using AI to Benefit Society

We use AI to help people.
We hope the positive outcome balances the negative impact on environment.

Promoting Responsible Use

We encourage everyone to use our platform responsibly.
By promoting careful use, we hope to lower unnecessary energy use and cut down our carbon footprint.

Offsetting Carbon Emissions
