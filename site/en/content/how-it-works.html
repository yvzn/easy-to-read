How EasyRead.me Works

Text Analysis

EasyRead.me looks at your text using smart technology.
It finds words that are hard to understand, long sentences, and difficult terms.

Simplification Process

When the analysis is done, we use a large language model to:

  • Replace hard words with easier ones
  • Break long sentences into shorter ones
  • Explain difficult terms in simple words
  • Keep the original meaning while making it easier to read

Checking for Improvement

Finally, we check each simplified text for:

  • Improvements in readability
  • Order of information
